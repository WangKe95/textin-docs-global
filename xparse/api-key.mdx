Click the link to get your API Key: [TextIn Console - Account & Developer Info](https://www.textin.com/console/dashboard/setting)

1. Click the profile icon in the bottom left corner of the feature experience page
2. Click "Account & Developer Info" to enter the corresponding console page

![AP Ikey 1 Pn](/images/APIkey-1.png)

3. Click the "Copy" icon to obtain your x-ti-app-id and x-ti-secret-code
